## Official Text

§ 7270. Definition of Insurance Company.

(a)   For the purposes of these regulations, insurance company shall mean any person that is
      subject to the California Insurance Code and its regulations. Insurance company shall
      include insurance institutions, agents, and insurance-support organizations, as those
      terms are defined in Insurance Code, section 791.02.
