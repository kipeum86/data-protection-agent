## Official Text

§ 7153. Additional Requirements for Businesses that Process Personal Information to Train
Automated Decisionmaking Technology.

(a)   A business that makes ADMT available to another business (“recipient-business”) to make
      a significant decision as set forth in section 7150, subsection (b)(3), must provide to the
      recipient-business all facts available to the business that are necessary for the recipient-
      business to conduct its own risk assessment.

(b)   The requirements of this section apply only to ADMT trained using personal information.
