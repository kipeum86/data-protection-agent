## Official Text

§ 7154. Goal of a Risk Assessment.

(a)   The goal of a risk assessment is restricting or prohibiting the processing of personal
      information if the risks to privacy of the consumer outweigh the benefits resulting from
      processing to the consumer, the business, other stakeholders, and the public.
