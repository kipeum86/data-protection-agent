## Official Text

1798.199.45.

(a)   Upon the sworn complaint of any person or on its own initiative, the agency may
      investigate possible violations of this title relating to any business, service provider,
      contractor, or person. The agency may decide not to investigate a complaint or decide to
      provide a business with a time period to cure the alleged violation. In making a decision
      not to investigate or provide more time to cure, the agency may consider the following:

      (1)   Lack of intent to violate this title.

      (2)   Voluntary efforts undertaken by the business, service provider, contractor, or person
            to cure the alleged violation prior to being notified by the agency of the complaint.

(b)   (1)   The agency shall notify in writing the person who made the complaint of the action,
            if any, the agency has taken or plans to take on the complaint, together with the
            reasons for that action or nonaction.

      (2)   The written notification required by this subdivision shall not include information
            that is subject to law enforcement exemptions and privileges, including, but not
            limited to, confidential information related to an investigation and information that
            is privileged under the Evidence Code and the Government Code.
