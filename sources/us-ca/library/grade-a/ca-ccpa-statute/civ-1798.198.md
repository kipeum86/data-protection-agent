## Official Text

1798.198.

(a)   Subject to limitation provided in subdivision (b), and in Section 1798.199, this title shall be
      operative January 1, 2020.

(b)   This title shall become operative only if initiative measure No. 17-0039, The Consumer
      Right to Privacy Act of 2018, is withdrawn from the ballot pursuant to Section 9604 of the
      Elections Code.
