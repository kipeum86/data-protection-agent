## Official Text

1798.199.100.

The agency and any court, as applicable, shall consider the good faith cooperation of the
business, service provider, contractor, or other person in determining the amount of any
administrative fine or civil penalty for a violation of this title. A business shall not be required by
the agency, a court, or otherwise to pay both an administrative fine and a civil penalty for the
same violation.
