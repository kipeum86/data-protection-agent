## C-456/22 — Gemeinde Ummendorf (Damages Quantum)

**Parties:** Gemeinde Ummendorf (Damages for Unlawful Processing)

**Judgment Date:** 2024-06-20

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:488

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

Building on the Österreichische Post damages line of cases, the CJEU provided further guidance on how national courts should assess the quantum of non-material damages under Art. 82 GDPR. The Court emphasized that while Member States retain procedural autonomy in determining damages amounts, the compensation must be full and effective, and courts cannot apply de minimis thresholds.
