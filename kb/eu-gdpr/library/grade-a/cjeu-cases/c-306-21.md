## C-306/21 — Koalitsia Demokratichna Bulgaria (Bulgarian Elections)

**Parties:** Koalitsia Demokratichna Bulgaria - Obedinenie v Predsedatel na Narodnoto sabranie na Republika Bulgaria

**Judgment Date:** 2023-11-16

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:854

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU examined the processing of personal identification numbers and biometric data in the context of electronic voting systems. It held that while ensuring electoral integrity is a legitimate public interest objective, the processing of such sensitive data must comply with strict GDPR safeguards and proportionality, including specific national legislation meeting Art. 9(2)(g) requirements.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 6(1)(e), Art. 9, Art. 87
