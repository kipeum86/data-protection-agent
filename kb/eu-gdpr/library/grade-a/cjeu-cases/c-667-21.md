## C-667/21 — Krankenversicherung Nordrhein

**Parties:** ZQ v Medizinischer Dienst der Krankenversicherung Nordrhein

**Judgment Date:** 2023-05-21

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:396

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU examined the processing of health data under Art. 9(2)(h) GDPR in the employment context. The Court held that where a medical service processes an employee's health data for occupational health assessment, this must comply with the specific safeguards for health data processing. Unauthorized access to health data by personnel without medical confidentiality obligations constitutes a GDPR violation.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 9(2)(h), Art. 82
