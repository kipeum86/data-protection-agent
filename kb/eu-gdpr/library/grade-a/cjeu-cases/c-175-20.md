## C-175/20 — Valsts ieņēmumu dienests (SIA SS)

**Parties:** SIA 'SS' v Valsts ieņēmumu dienests

**Judgment Date:** 2022-02-24

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2022:124

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU ruled that a tax authority's request for bulk disclosure of personal data from an online advertising platform must comply with the data minimization principle. Requiring transmission of all data about all advertisements without any criteria related to the tax inquiry objective is disproportionate. The decision set important limits on public authority data requests.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 5(1)(c), Art. 6(1)(c), Art. 6(1)(e)
