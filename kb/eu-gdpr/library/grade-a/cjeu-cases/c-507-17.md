## C-507/17 — Google LLC (Territorial Scope of De-referencing)

**Parties:** Google LLC v Commission nationale de l'informatique et des libertés (CNIL)

**Judgment Date:** 2019-09-24

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2019:772

**Significance:** landmark

### Key Holding

The CJEU ruled that the right to de-referencing under EU law does not require a search engine operator to carry out de-referencing on all its domain name versions globally. However, it must dereference on all EU Member State versions and take measures to discourage EU-based users from accessing delisted links on non-EU domains. This case defined the geographic boundaries of the right to erasure.
