## C-394/23 — Mousse (Right to Compensation and Controller Identification)

**Parties:** Mousse v Commission nationale de l'informatique et des libertés (CNIL) and Collecte Localisation Satellites (CLS)

**Judgment Date:** 2024-11-14

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:910

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU clarified the relationship between the data subject's right to lodge a complaint with a DPA (Art. 77), the right to judicial remedy against a DPA (Art. 78), and the right to compensation (Art. 82). It confirmed that these remedies are complementary and can be exercised in parallel, and that a DPA's decision not to act on a complaint does not preclude a separate compensation claim.
