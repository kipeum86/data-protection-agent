## C-118/22 — Gemeinde Ummendorf

**Parties:** Gemeinde Ummendorf v Land Baden-Württemberg

**Judgment Date:** 2024-02-08

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:97

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU addressed when public authorities must designate a DPO under Art. 37(1)(a) GDPR. The Court provided guidance on the obligation for all public authorities, including small municipalities, to appoint a DPO, and clarified that Member States may set additional criteria under Art. 37(4) for mandatory DPO appointment.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 37, Art. 38, Art. 39
