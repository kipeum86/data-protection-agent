## C-645/19 — Facebook Ireland (Belgian DPA)

**Parties:** Facebook Ireland Ltd, Facebook Inc., Facebook Belgium BVBA v Gegevensbeschermingsautoriteit

**Judgment Date:** 2021-06-15

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2021:483

**Significance:** landmark

### Key Holding

The CJEU clarified the one-stop-shop mechanism under GDPR, holding that a non-lead supervisory authority may exercise its power to bring proceedings before a national court for cross-border processing in specific circumstances defined in Art. 56(2) and Art. 66 GDPR. This case resolved critical questions about the allocation of DPA powers in cross-border cases.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 55, Art. 56, Art. 58, Art. 60-66
