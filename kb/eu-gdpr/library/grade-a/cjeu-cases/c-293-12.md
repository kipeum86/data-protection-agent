## C-293/12 — Digital Rights Ireland

**Parties:** Digital Rights Ireland Ltd v Minister for Communications, Marine and Natural Resources and Others; Kärntner Landesregierung and Others

**Judgment Date:** 2014-04-08

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2014:238

**Significance:** landmark

### Key Holding

The CJEU invalidated the Data Retention Directive 2006/24/EC as incompatible with Articles 7 and 8 of the EU Charter of Fundamental Rights. The Court held that general and indiscriminate retention of traffic and location data constitutes a particularly serious interference with fundamental rights, establishing a proportionality benchmark that continues to shape GDPR enforcement and data minimization principles.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 5, Art. 6
