## C-604/22 — IAB Europe (TCF)

**Parties:** IAB Europe v Gegevensbeschermingsautoriteit

**Judgment Date:** 2024-03-07

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:111

**Significance:** landmark

### Key Holding

The CJEU ruled that the TC String used in IAB Europe's Transparency and Consent Framework constitutes personal data as it can be linked to identifiable users. IAB Europe qualifies as a joint controller with participating organizations, even though it does not itself have direct access to the raw personal data. This decision has massive implications for the entire programmatic advertising ecosystem and consent management platforms.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 4(1), Art. 4(7), Art. 26
