## C-333/22 — Ligue des droits humains (PNR)

**Parties:** Ligue des droits humains ASBL v Autorité belge de protection des données

**Judgment Date:** 2024-04-11

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:222

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

Following its landmark 2022 PNR ruling (C-817/19), the CJEU provided further guidance on the proportionality of passenger name record data processing systems. The Court confirmed that systematic processing of PNR data must be limited to what is strictly necessary and that retention periods must be proportionate, applying GDPR principles even to EU PNR Directive processing.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 5, Art. 6
