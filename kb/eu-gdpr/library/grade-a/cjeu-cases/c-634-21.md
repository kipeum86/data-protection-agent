## C-634/21 — SCHUFA II (Data Retention from Public Registers)

**Parties:** SCHUFA Holding AG (Retention of insolvency data)

**Judgment Date:** 2023-12-07

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:987

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU ruled that a private credit agency's practice of storing personal insolvency data longer than the public insolvency register itself is unlawful under the GDPR, unless the controller can demonstrate the necessity for such extended storage. This established important limits on how long private entities may retain data originally sourced from public registers.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 5(1)(e), Art. 6(1)(f), Art. 17
