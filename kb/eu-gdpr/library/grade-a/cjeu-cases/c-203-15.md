## C-203/15 — Tele2 Sverige

**Parties:** Tele2 Sverige AB v Post- och telestyrelsen; Secretary of State for the Home Department v Tom Watson and Others

**Judgment Date:** 2016-12-21

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2016:970

**Significance:** landmark

### Key Holding

Following Digital Rights Ireland, the CJEU confirmed that EU law precludes national legislation requiring general and indiscriminate retention of traffic and location data. The Court elaborated that targeted retention may be permissible where strictly necessary, establishing key principles on the proportionality of data processing that directly inform GDPR data minimization and purpose limitation.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 5, Art. 6
