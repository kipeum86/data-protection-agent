## C-65/23 — K GmbH (Employee Monitoring)

**Parties:** K GmbH

**Judgment Date:** 2024-06-13

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:469

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU examined the balancing test required under Art. 6(1)(f) for employee monitoring scenarios and the interplay with the right to object under Art. 21. The Court clarified that where an employee successfully exercises the right to object, the controller must cease processing and erase the data unless compelling legitimate grounds override the data subject's interests.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 6(1)(f), Art. 17, Art. 21
