## C-178/22 — Procura della Repubblica (Criminal Proceedings and GDPR)

**Parties:** Procura della Repubblica presso il Tribunale di Bolzano

**Judgment Date:** 2024-01-25

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:44

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU clarified the boundary between GDPR and the Law Enforcement Directive (LED) 2016/680 regarding data processing in criminal proceedings. The Court held that the GDPR exclusion in Art. 2(2)(d) applies only to processing by competent authorities for law enforcement purposes as defined in the LED, and that ancillary administrative processing may still fall under GDPR.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 2(2)(d), Art. 6(1)(c)
