## C-807/21 — Deutsche Wohnen

**Parties:** Deutsche Wohnen SE v Staatsanwaltschaft Berlin

**Judgment Date:** 2023-12-05

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:950

**Significance:** landmark

### Key Holding

The CJEU ruled that administrative fines under Art. 83 GDPR can be imposed directly on an undertaking (legal person) without needing to first identify a specific natural person who committed the infringement. However, fines require at least negligent conduct — strict liability is not permitted. This landmark ruling resolved a major uncertainty about corporate GDPR fine liability across the EU.
