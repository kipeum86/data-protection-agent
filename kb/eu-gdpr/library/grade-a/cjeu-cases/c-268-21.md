## C-268/21 — Norra Stockholm Bygg (Disclosure in Litigation)

**Parties:** Norra Stockholm Bygg AB and Others v Per Nycander AB

**Judgment Date:** 2023-03-02

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:145

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU held that GDPR must be taken into account when a national court orders disclosure of documents containing personal data in civil proceedings. Courts must balance the right to effective judicial protection against data protection rights, applying data minimization to limit disclosure to what is strictly necessary. This case clarified the intersection between civil procedure and GDPR.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 5(1)(c), Art. 6(1)(f)
