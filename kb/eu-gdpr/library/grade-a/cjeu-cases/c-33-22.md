## C-33/22 — Österreichische Datenschutzbehörde (Linz Municipality)

**Parties:** Österreichische Datenschutzbehörde v Gemeinde Linz

**Judgment Date:** 2024-01-25

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:46

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU addressed the DPO conflict of interest prohibition under Art. 38(6) GDPR, holding that a person who simultaneously determines the purposes and means of processing cannot serve as DPO. It also confirmed that Member States may exclude public authorities from administrative fines under Art. 83(7) but must still ensure effective enforcement.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 37, Art. 38, Art. 83
