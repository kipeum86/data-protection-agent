## C-579/21 — Pankki S

**Parties:** J.M. v Pankki S

**Judgment Date:** 2024-01-25

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:45

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU held that the data subject's right of access under Art. 15 GDPR is not conditional on the data subject stating the reasons for the request. A controller cannot refuse access merely because the request is motivated by purposes unrelated to data protection. The right of access exists regardless of the purpose for which it is exercised, though manifestly unfounded or excessive requests may be refused under Art. 12(5).

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 15, Art. 12(5)
