## C-683/21 — Nacionalinis visuomenės sveikatos centras

**Parties:** Nacionalinis visuomenės sveikatos centras prie Sveikatos apsaugos ministerijos v Valstybinė duomenų apsaugos inspekcija

**Judgment Date:** 2023-12-07

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:949

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU clarified that joint controllership can arise from participation in determining the purposes and means of processing, even without a formal agreement. It held that a DPA may fine a controller for processing carried out by a processor on its behalf. Crucially, the Court confirmed that fault (intentional or negligent conduct) is required for imposing administrative fines under Art. 83 GDPR.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 4(7), Art. 26, Art. 82, Art. 83
