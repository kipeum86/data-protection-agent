## C-34/21 — Hauptpersonalrat der Lehrerinnen und Lehrer

**Parties:** Hauptpersonalrat der Lehrerinnen und Lehrer beim Hessischen Kultusministerium

**Judgment Date:** 2022-11-22

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2022:905

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU addressed whether teacher consent is required for live-streamed video lessons. The Court held that processing of teachers' personal data through video conferencing for educational purposes during COVID could be based on Art. 6(1)(e) (public interest), without requiring individual consent, provided adequate safeguards are in place.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 6(1)(e), Art. 88
