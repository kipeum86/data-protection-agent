## C-461/20 — Asociación Estatal de Entidades de Servicios de Atención a Domicilio (ADAD)

**Parties:** Asociación Estatal de Entidades de Servicios de Atención a Domicilio v Diputación Foral de Gipuzkoa

**Judgment Date:** 2022-08-01

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2022:592

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU assessed whether GDPR precludes national legislation requiring public procurement tenderers to disclose employee identity data. It held that while transparency in public procurement may justify data processing, the principle of proportionality requires that only data necessary for the legitimate objective be disclosed, reinforcing GDPR data minimization even in public contract contexts.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 6(1)(c), Art. 6(1)(e), Art. 9
