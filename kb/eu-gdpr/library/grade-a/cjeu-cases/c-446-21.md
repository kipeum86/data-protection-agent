## C-446/21 — Schrems III (Facebook Meta)

**Parties:** Maximillian Schrems v Meta Platforms Ireland Ltd

**Judgment Date:** 2024-10-04

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:957

**Significance:** landmark

### Key Holding

The CJEU ruled that the principle of data minimization under Art. 5(1)(c) GDPR precludes a social network platform from using all personal data obtained for targeted advertising purposes without restriction as to time. The Court also held that where a data subject publicly reveals their sexual orientation, this does not authorize processing of other data concerning sexual orientation via automated means for targeted advertising.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 5(1)(c), Art. 6(1)(b), Art. 9
