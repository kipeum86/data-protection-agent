## C-460/20 — Google (Accurate De-referencing)

**Parties:** TU and RE v Google LLC

**Judgment Date:** 2022-12-08

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2022:962

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU held that a search engine operator must dereference content displayed in search results, including thumbnail images, where the information is manifestly inaccurate. The data subject does not need to obtain a prior court order against the content publisher; they must merely demonstrate that the claim is manifestly unfounded. This strengthened the right to erasure in relation to search engine content presentation.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 17, Art. 5(1)(d)
