## C-184/20 — Vyriausioji tarnybinės etikos komisija

**Parties:** OT v Vyriausioji tarnybinės etikos komisija

**Judgment Date:** 2022-08-01

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2022:601

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU held that national legislation requiring online publication of personal data from declarations of interest for anti-corruption purposes must comply with the proportionality principle under GDPR. The online publication of all such data, including sensitive data about cohabitants, was disproportionate. This case established important balancing criteria between public interest transparency and data protection.
