## C-740/22 — Endemol Shine (Reality TV)

**Parties:** Endemol Shine Finland Oy

**Judgment Date:** 2024-10-04

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:795

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU addressed GDPR consent requirements in the context of reality television production, including whether disclosure of health-related information by participants constitutes valid consent for broadcast processing of special category data. The Court clarified conditions for valid consent under Art. 7 in media contexts and the scope of the journalistic exemption under Art. 85.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 6(1)(a), Art. 7, Art. 9
