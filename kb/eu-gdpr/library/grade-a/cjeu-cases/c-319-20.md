## C-319/20 — Meta Platforms Ireland (Facebook Fan Pages - Sequel)

**Parties:** Meta Platforms Ireland Ltd v Bundesverband der Verbraucherzentralen und Verbraucherverbände

**Judgment Date:** 2022-04-28

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2022:322

**Significance:** landmark

### Key Holding

The CJEU held that consumer protection associations may bring representative actions against data protection infringements under Art. 80(2) GDPR, even without a specific mandate from a data subject and irrespective of actual infringement of specific data subjects' rights. This landmark ruling enabled collective GDPR enforcement by consumer organizations across the EU.
