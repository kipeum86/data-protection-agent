## C-200/23 — Agentsia po vpisvaniyata (Agency for Registrations)

**Parties:** Agentsia po vpisvaniyata

**Judgment Date:** 2024-10-17

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:827

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU held that the principle of data minimization and data protection by design under GDPR applies to the disclosure of personal data in commercial/company registers. Even where EU or national law mandates publication, the data disclosed must be limited to what is necessary, and access mechanisms should incorporate proportionality safeguards.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 5(1)(c), Art. 6(1)(c), Art. 6(1)(e), Art. 25
