## Opinion 28/2022 — Opinion on the Europrivacy criteria of certification regarding their approval as European Data Protection Seal pursuant to Article 42.5 GDPR

**Published:** 2022-10-10

### Summary

Approved Europrivacy as the first-ever European Data Protection Seal under Art. 42(5), enabling a scalable certification mechanism with EU-wide validity for controllers and processors across all sectors.
