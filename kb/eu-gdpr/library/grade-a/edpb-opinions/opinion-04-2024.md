## Opinion 04/2024 — Opinion on the notion of main establishment of a controller in the Union under Art. 4.16(a) GDPR

**Published:** 2024-02-13

### Summary

Clarified that a controller's place of central administration qualifies as a main establishment only if it takes decisions on purposes/means of processing and has the power to implement them; if decisions are made outside the EU, the one-stop-shop mechanism does not apply.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 4(16)(a), Art. 56, Art. 64(2)
