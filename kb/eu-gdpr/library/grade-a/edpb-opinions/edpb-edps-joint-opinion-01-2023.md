## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 01/2023 — Joint Opinion on the Proposal for a Regulation laying down additional procedural rules relating to the enforcement of Regulation (EU) 2016/679

**Published:** 2023-09-19

### Summary

Strongly supported the Commission's proposal to harmonise GDPR cross-border enforcement procedures, welcoming new procedural steps for faster cooperation while recommending further strengthening of complainants' rights and investigation timelines.

Art. 60, Art. 63, Art. 65, Art. 66
