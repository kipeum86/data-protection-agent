## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 03/2021 — Joint Opinion on the Proposal for a Regulation on European data governance (Data Governance Act)

**Published:** 2021-03-10

### Summary

Assessed the Data Governance Act proposal, stressing that GDPR must remain fully applicable, and raising concerns about the conditions for re-use of public sector data containing personal data and the role of data intermediaries.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 6, Art. 9, Art. 46, Art. 49
