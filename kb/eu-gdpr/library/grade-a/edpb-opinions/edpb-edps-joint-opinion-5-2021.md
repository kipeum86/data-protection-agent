## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 5/2021 — Joint Opinion on the proposal for a Regulation laying down harmonised rules on artificial intelligence (Artificial Intelligence Act)

**Published:** 2021-06-18

### Summary

Called for a general ban on AI-based remote biometric identification in publicly accessible spaces and recommended that DPAs be designated as national supervisory authorities for the AI Act to ensure coherent enforcement with GDPR.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 22, Art. 35, Art. 9
