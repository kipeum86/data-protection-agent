## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 1/2026 — Joint Opinion on the Proposal for a Regulation as regards the simplification of the implementation of harmonised rules on artificial intelligence (Digital Omnibus on AI)

**Published:** 2026-01-21

### Summary

Supported streamlining AI Act implementation but warned that proposed relaxations of fundamental rights impact assessments and expanded regulatory sandbox provisions could undermine safeguards protecting personal data in AI systems.

Art. 22, Art. 35, Art. 9
