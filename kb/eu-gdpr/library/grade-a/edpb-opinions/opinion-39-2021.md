## Opinion 39/2021 — Opinion on whether Article 58(2)(g) GDPR could serve as a legal basis for a supervisory authority to order ex officio the erasure of personal data

**Published:** 2021-12-14

### Summary

Clarified that DPAs may use their corrective power under Art. 58(2)(g) to order erasure of personal data ex officio even without a data subject's request, strengthening DPAs' enforcement toolkit.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 58(2)(g), Art. 17, Art. 64(2)
