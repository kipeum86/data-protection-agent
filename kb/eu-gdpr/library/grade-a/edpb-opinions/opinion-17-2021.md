## Opinion 17/2021 — Opinion on the draft decision of the French Supervisory Authority regarding the European code of conduct submitted by the Cloud Infrastructure Service Providers (CISPE)

**Published:** 2021-05-19

### Summary

Approved the CISPE Code of Conduct specifically for cloud infrastructure service providers (IaaS), complementing the EU Cloud CoC by addressing the specific data protection obligations of infrastructure-level processors.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 40, Art. 41, Art. 64(1)(b)
