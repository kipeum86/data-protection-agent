## Opinion 5/2019 — Opinion on the interplay between the ePrivacy Directive and the GDPR, in particular regarding the competence, tasks and powers of data protection authorities

**Published:** 2019-03-12

### Summary

Established the lex generalis/lex specialis relationship between the GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive, clarifying that DPAs are competent to enforce GDPR requirements that apply to processing operations triggered by ePrivacy rules (e.g., cookies and tracking).

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 64(2), Art. 95
