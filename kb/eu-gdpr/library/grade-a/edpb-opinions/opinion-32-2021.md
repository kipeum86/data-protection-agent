## Opinion 32/2021 — Opinion regarding the European Commission Draft Implementing Decision on the adequate protection of personal data in the Republic of Korea

**Published:** 2021-09-28

### Summary

Assessed South Korea's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA), welcoming convergence with GDPR while requesting clarification on binding nature of supplementary rules and access for national security purposes.
