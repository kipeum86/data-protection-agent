## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 03/2022 — Joint Opinion on the Proposal for a Regulation on the European Health Data Space

**Published:** 2022-07-12

### Summary

Assessed the EHDS proposal, warning that derogations from GDPR provisions (especially Art. 14) for health data access bodies undermine data subjects' control over their personal data, and calling for clearer purpose limitations for secondary use.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 6, Art. 9, Art. 14, Art. 89
