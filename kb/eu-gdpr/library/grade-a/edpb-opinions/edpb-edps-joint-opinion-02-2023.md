## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 02/2023 — Joint Opinion on the Proposal for a Regulation on the establishment of the digital euro

**Published:** 2023-10-18

### Summary

Recommended mandatory pseudonymisation of all digital euro transaction data vis-a-vis the ECB/central banks and the introduction of a privacy threshold for low-value online transactions to maintain cash-like privacy levels in digital payments.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 5, Art. 6, Art. 25, Art. 32
