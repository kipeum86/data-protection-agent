## Opinion 26/2025 — Opinion regarding the European Commission Draft Implementing Decision on the adequate protection of personal data in the United Kingdom (GDPR) - Renewal

**Published:** 2025-10-07

### Summary

Assessed the renewal of the UK adequacy decision considering the UK's new Data Use and Access Act and the Investigatory Powers Amendment Act 2024, evaluating whether the UK continues to provide an essentially equivalent level of data protection.
