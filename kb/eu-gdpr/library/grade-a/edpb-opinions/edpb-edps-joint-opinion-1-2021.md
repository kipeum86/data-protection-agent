## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 1/2021 — Joint Opinion on standard contractual clauses between controllers and processors

**Published:** 2021-01-14

### Summary

Provided the EDPB/EDPS assessment on the European Commission's draft standard contractual clauses for controller-processor relationships under Art. 28(7), aiming to achieve EU-wide harmonisation of data processing agreements.
