## Opinion 25/2022 — Opinion regarding the European Privacy Seal (EuroPriSe) certification criteria for the certification of processing operations by processors

**Published:** 2022-09-19

### Summary

Approved the EuroPriSe certification criteria as one of the first GDPR certification mechanisms, while clarifying that such certification does not itself constitute a valid data transfer mechanism under Art. 46(2)(f).

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 42, Art. 42(5), Art. 64(1)(c)
