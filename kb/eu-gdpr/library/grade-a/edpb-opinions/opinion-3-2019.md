## Opinion 3/2019 — Opinion concerning the Questions and Answers on the interplay between the Clinical Trials Regulation (CTR) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

**Published:** 2019-01-23

### Summary

Addressed the interplay between the Clinical Trials Regulation and GDPR, clarifying legal bases for processing health data in clinical trials and the role of consent under both regulatory frameworks.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 6, Art. 9, Art. 70(1)(b)
