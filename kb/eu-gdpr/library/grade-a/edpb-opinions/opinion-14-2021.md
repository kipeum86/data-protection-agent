## Opinion 14/2021 — Opinion regarding the European Commission Draft Implementing Decision on the adequate protection of personal data in the United Kingdom (GDPR)

**Published:** 2021-04-13

### Summary

Assessed UK's post-Brexit data protection framework, recognising substantial equivalence with GDPR while expressing significant concerns about bulk data interception powers, exemptions for immigration data, and potential future divergence.

Art. 45, Art. 70(1)(s)
