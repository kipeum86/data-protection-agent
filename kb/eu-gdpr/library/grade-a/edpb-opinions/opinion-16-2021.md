## Opinion 16/2021 — Opinion on the draft decision of the Belgian Supervisory Authority regarding the EU Cloud Code of Conduct submitted by SCOPE Europe

**Published:** 2021-05-19

### Summary

Approved the first transnational GDPR Code of Conduct for cloud service providers submitted by SCOPE Europe, establishing a compliance framework for cloud processors across the EU and setting precedent for future codes.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 40, Art. 41, Art. 64(1)(b)
