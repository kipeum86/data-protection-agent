## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 04/2022 — Joint Opinion on the Proposal for a Regulation laying down rules to prevent and combat child sexual abuse (CSAM)

**Published:** 2022-07-28

### Summary

Expressed serious concerns that the CSAM detection proposal risks becoming the basis for generalised and indiscriminate scanning of electronic communications, finding that detection orders for unknown CSAM and grooming in private messages are disproportionate and threaten encryption.

Art. 5, Art. 6, Art. 9, Art. 22
