## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 2/2022 — Joint Opinion on the Proposal of the European Parliament and of the Council on harmonised rules on fair access to and use of data (Data Act)

**Published:** 2022-05-04

### Summary

Assessed the Data Act proposal, welcoming enhanced data portability for IoT users while expressing deep concerns about the proportionality of mandatory data disclosure to public sector bodies and the need to preserve GDPR primacy.

Art. 6, Art. 9, Art. 20
