## Opinion 28/2018 — Opinion regarding the European Commission Draft Implementing Decision on the adequate protection of personal data in Japan

**Published:** 2018-12-05

### Summary

The EDPB's first adequacy opinion under GDPR assessed Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI), finding general alignment with GDPR while noting areas for monitoring, setting the template for all subsequent adequacy assessments.

Art. 45, Art. 70(1)(s)
