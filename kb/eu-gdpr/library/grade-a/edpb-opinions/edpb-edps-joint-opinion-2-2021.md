## EDPB-EDPS Joint Opinion 2/2021 — Joint Opinion on standard contractual clauses for the transfer of personal data to third countries

**Published:** 2021-01-14

### Summary

Assessed the Commission's new SCCs for international data transfers post-Schrems II, introducing a modular approach covering four transfer scenarios and the requirement for transfer impact assessments.

Art. 46(2)(c), Art. 46(3)(a), Chapter V
