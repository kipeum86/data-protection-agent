## Binding Decision 1/2023 — Binding Decision 1/2023

**Target Entity:** Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (Facebook service)

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2023-04-13

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** International data transfers: Meta's continued transfers of personal data from the EU/EEA to the United States on the basis of standard contractual clauses (SCCs) following the CJEU Schrems II judgment, without implementing adequate supplementary measures.

### Summary

The EDPB found that Meta's systematic, repetitive, and continuous transfers of EU personal data to the US violated Art. 46(1) GDPR, as SCCs alone did not provide adequate safeguards against US government surveillance. This resulted in the largest-ever GDPR fine of EUR 1.2 billion and an order to cease unlawful transfers within six months.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 46(1), Art. 46(2)(c), Chapter V
