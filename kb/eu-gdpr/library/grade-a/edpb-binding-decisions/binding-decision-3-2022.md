## Binding Decision 3/2022 — Binding Decision 3/2022

**Target Entity:** Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (Facebook service)

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2022-12-05

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** Lawfulness and transparency of processing personal data for behavioural advertising. Whether Meta could rely on 'performance of contract' (Art. 6(1)(b)) as a legal basis for processing personal data for behavioural advertising through its Facebook service.

### Summary

The EDPB determined that Meta could not rely on 'performance of contract' as a legal basis for processing personal data for behavioural advertising on Facebook, as targeted advertising is not a core element of the service. The Irish DPC imposed a EUR 210 million fine and ordered Meta to bring processing into compliance within three months.
