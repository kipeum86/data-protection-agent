## Binding Decision 1/2021 — Binding Decision 1/2021

**Target Entity:** WhatsApp Ireland Limited

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2021-07-28

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** Transparency obligations: WhatsApp failed to provide adequate information to users and non-users about the processing of their personal data. Dispute on classification of data as personal data, adequacy of transparency information, and calculation of the fine.

### Summary

The EDPB found WhatsApp breached transparency obligations by failing to provide adequate information to both users and non-users, instructing the Irish DPC to significantly increase the fine from EUR 50 million to EUR 225 million. This decision established important precedent on transparency requirements and the calculation methodology for administrative fines.
