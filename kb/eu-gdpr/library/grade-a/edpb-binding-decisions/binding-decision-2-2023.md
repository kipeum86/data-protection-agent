## Binding Decision 2/2023 — Binding Decision 2/2023

**Target Entity:** TikTok Technology Limited

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2023-08-02

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** Processing of children's personal data (users aged 13-17) in connection with deceptive design practices. Two pop-ups (Registration Pop-Up and Video Posting Pop-Up) used manipulative design to nudge children into privacy-unfriendly choices. Age verification adequacy.

### Summary

The EDPB found that TikTok's design practices (deceptive pop-ups encouraging children to skip registration and post videos) violated the GDPR fairness principle and data protection by design/default. A EUR 345 million fine was imposed, establishing landmark precedent on the application of the fairness principle to dark patterns targeting minors.
