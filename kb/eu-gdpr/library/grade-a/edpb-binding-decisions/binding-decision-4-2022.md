## Binding Decision 4/2022 — Binding Decision 4/2022

**Target Entity:** Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (Instagram service)

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2022-12-05

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** Lawfulness and transparency of processing personal data for behavioural advertising on Instagram. Same core issue as BD 3/2022 applied to the Instagram service: whether 'performance of contract' can serve as legal basis for behavioural advertising.

### Summary

Paralleling BD 3/2022 for Facebook, the EDPB found that Meta could not use 'performance of contract' as legal basis for processing personal data for behavioural advertising on Instagram. The Irish DPC imposed a EUR 180 million fine, and together with the Facebook decision, this effectively ended Meta's reliance on contract as a basis for ad targeting in the EU.
