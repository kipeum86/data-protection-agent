## Urgent Binding Decision 01/2023 — Urgent Binding Decision 01/2023

**Target Entity:** Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (Facebook & Instagram)

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2023-10-27

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** EEA-wide ban on processing personal data for behavioural advertising on the legal bases of contract and legitimate interest. First use of the Art. 66(2) urgency procedure by the EDPB to impose final measures across the entire EEA.

### Summary

Following a request from the Norwegian DPA under Art. 66(2), the EDPB adopted its first urgent binding decision instructing the Irish DPC to impose an EEA-wide ban on Meta's processing of personal data for behavioural advertising based on contract or legitimate interest within two weeks. This historic decision effectively forced Meta to switch to consent-based advertising across Europe.
