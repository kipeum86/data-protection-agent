## Decision 01/2020 — Decision 01/2020

**Target Entity:** Twitter International Company

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2020-11-09

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** Data breach notification obligations under Article 33 GDPR; Twitter failed to notify the DPC of a personal data breach within 72 hours. Dispute arose over scope of infringements found and the adequacy of the proposed fine.

### Summary

The EDPB's first-ever Art. 65 binding decision resolved a dispute over Twitter's failure to timely notify a data breach involving a bug that exposed protected tweets. The EDPB instructed the Irish DPC to reassess the proposed fine and include additional infringement findings, resulting in a EUR 450,000 fine.
