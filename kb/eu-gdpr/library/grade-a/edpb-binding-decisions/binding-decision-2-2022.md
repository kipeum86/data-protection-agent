## Binding Decision 2/2022 — Binding Decision 2/2022

**Target Entity:** Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (Instagram)

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2022-07-28

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** Processing of children's personal data on Instagram, including public disclosure of email addresses and phone numbers of child users of the Instagram business account feature, and public-by-default settings for children's personal accounts. Lawfulness of processing under Art. 6(1).

### Summary

The EDPB's first binding decision addressing the lawfulness of processing under Art. 6, finding that Instagram had no valid legal basis for processing children's personal data and that 'performance of contract' was not applicable. A record fine of EUR 405 million was imposed by the DPC following the EDPB's instruction to find additional Art. 6(1) infringements.
