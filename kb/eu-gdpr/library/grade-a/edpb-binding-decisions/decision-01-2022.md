## Decision 01/2022 — Decision 01/2022

**Target Entity:** Accor SA

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2022-06-15

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** Calculation of administrative fines: dispute over whether the fine was sufficiently dissuasive and which financial year's turnover should be used as the reference for computing the maximum fine. Also addressed the right to object to direct marketing and the right of access.

### Summary

The EDPB ruled that the CNIL must use the preceding year's turnover (2021) for calculating the maximum fine and that the original EUR 100,000 fine was not sufficiently dissuasive. The fine was increased to EUR 600,000, establishing an important precedent on fine calculation methodology including the relevant reference year for turnover.
