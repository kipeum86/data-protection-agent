## Binding Decision 5/2022 — Binding Decision 5/2022

**Target Entity:** WhatsApp Ireland Limited

**Fine Amount:** 

**Decision Date:** 2022-12-05

**DPAs Involved:** 

**Key Issue:** Lawfulness of processing personal data for service improvement and safety/security purposes. Whether WhatsApp could rely on 'performance of contract' or 'legitimate interest' for processing personal data for purposes beyond the core messaging service.

### Summary

The EDPB found that WhatsApp unlawfully processed personal data for service improvement and safety/security purposes without a valid legal basis and in an unfair manner. A EUR 5.5 million fine was imposed, completing the trio of December 2022 decisions that comprehensively addressed Meta's data processing practices across its three major platforms.
