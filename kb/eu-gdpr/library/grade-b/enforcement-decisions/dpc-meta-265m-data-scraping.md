## DPC v Meta (Facebook) — EUR 265 Million

**DPA:** DPC (Ireland)

**Target Entity:** Meta Platforms Ireland Limited

**Fine Amount:** EUR 265,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2022-11-25

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

DPC fined Meta EUR 265M after 533 million users' data appeared on a hacking forum in April 2021, scraped via Facebook Search, Messenger Contact Importer, and Instagram Contact Importer tools. The decision found Meta failed to implement data protection by design and by default (Art. 25), establishing that controllers must proactively prevent scraping through technical measures.

### Violated Articles

Art. 25(1), Art. 25(2)
