## CNIL v Google — EUR 325 Million

**DPA:** CNIL (France)

**Target Entity:** Google LLC / Google Ireland Limited

**Fine Amount:** EUR 325,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2025-09-01

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

CNIL imposed a record EUR 325M fine on Google for displaying advertisements in Gmail inboxes without consent and placing cookies during account creation without valid consent. The decision is notable for treating recidivism as an aggravating factor, citing Google's prior cookie fines in 2020 (EUR 100M) and 2021 (EUR 150M). Affected 74 million accounts and 53 million individuals.

### Violated Articles

Art. 82 French Data Protection Act (ePrivacy transposition)
