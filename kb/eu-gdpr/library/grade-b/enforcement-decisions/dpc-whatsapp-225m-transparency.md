## DPC v WhatsApp — EUR 225 Million

**DPA:** DPC (Ireland)

**Target Entity:** WhatsApp Ireland Limited

**Fine Amount:** EUR 225,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2021-09-02

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

DPC fined WhatsApp EUR 225M for failing to provide transparent privacy information to both WhatsApp users and non-users whose data was processed. The EDPB invoked its dispute resolution mechanism to increase the fine from the DPC's initial proposal of EUR 30-50M, establishing a critical precedent for the binding dispute resolution process under Art. 65 GDPR.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1)(a), Art. 12, Art. 13, Art. 14
