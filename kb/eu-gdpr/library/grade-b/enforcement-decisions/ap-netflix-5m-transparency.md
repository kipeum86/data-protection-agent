## AP Netherlands v Netflix — EUR 4.75 Million

**DPA:** AP (Netherlands)

**Target Entity:** Netflix International B.V.

**Fine Amount:** EUR 4,750,000

**Decision Date:** 2024-12-18

**Significance:** important

### Summary

The Dutch DPA fined Netflix EUR 4.75M for failing to provide adequate transparency about personal data processing between 2018-2020 and inadequately responding to data subject access requests. Netflix's privacy statement did not clearly explain what data was collected and for what purposes. The decision reinforces that privacy policies must be concise, transparent, and in accessible plain language.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1)(a), Art. 5(2), Art. 12, Art. 13, Art. 15
