## CNIL v Google — EUR 150 Million (Cookies 2022)

**DPA:** CNIL (France)

**Target Entity:** Google LLC / Google Ireland Limited

**Fine Amount:** EUR 150,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2021-12-31

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

CNIL fined Google EUR 150M (EUR 90M to Google LLC, EUR 60M to Google Ireland) for making it difficult for users on google.fr and youtube.com to reject cookies compared to accepting them — users could accept in one click but required multiple steps to refuse. The decision, alongside a simultaneous EUR 60M fine against Facebook, established that cookie consent interfaces must offer symmetrical accept/reject options.

### Violated Articles

Art. 82 French Data Protection Act (ePrivacy transposition)
