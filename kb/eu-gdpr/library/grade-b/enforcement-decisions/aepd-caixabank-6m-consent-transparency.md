## AEPD v CaixaBank — EUR 6 Million

**DPA:** AEPD (Spain)

**Target Entity:** CaixaBank S.A.

**Fine Amount:** EUR 6,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2021-01-13

**Significance:** important

### Summary

The AEPD fined CaixaBank EUR 6M — EUR 4M for processing personal data without a proper legal basis (especially data processed under claimed legitimate interest) and EUR 2M for failing to provide sufficient information to data subjects under Articles 13-14. This was the AEPD's largest fine at the time and established important precedent for how financial institutions may rely on legitimate interest as a legal basis.

### Violated Articles

Art. 6, Art. 13, Art. 14
