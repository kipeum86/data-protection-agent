## DPC v Meta — EUR 91 Million

**DPA:** DPC (Ireland)

**Target Entity:** Meta Platforms Ireland Limited

**Fine Amount:** EUR 91,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2024-09-27

**Significance:** important

### Summary

DPC fined Meta EUR 91M after discovering that user passwords for Facebook and Instagram were stored in plaintext on internal systems without cryptographic protection. Meta failed to notify the DPC of the breach in a timely manner and failed to document it properly. The case highlights that even inadvertent security failures in password storage constitute GDPR violations.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1)(f), Art. 32(1), Art. 33(1), Art. 33(5)
