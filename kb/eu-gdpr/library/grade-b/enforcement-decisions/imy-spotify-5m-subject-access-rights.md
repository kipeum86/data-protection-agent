## IMY v Spotify — EUR 5 Million (SEK 58M)

**DPA:** IMY (Sweden)

**Target Entity:** Spotify AB

**Fine Amount:** SEK 58,000,000 (approx. EUR 5,000,000)

**Decision Date:** 2023-06-12

**Significance:** important

### Summary

Sweden's IMY fined Spotify SEK 58M for failing to adequately respond to data subject access requests under Art. 15. Spotify did not sufficiently explain the categories of personal data processed, the purposes, and how data in technical log files related to the individual. Originally filed by noyb in 2019, the case was upheld on appeal in June 2025 and clarifies that DSAR responses must be comprehensible, not merely technically complete.

### Violated Articles

Art. 12, Art. 15(1), Art. 15(2)
