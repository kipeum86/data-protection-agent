## CNIL v Amazon France Logistique — EUR 32 Million

**DPA:** CNIL (France)

**Target Entity:** Amazon France Logistique SAS

**Fine Amount:** EUR 32,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2023-12-27

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

CNIL fined Amazon EUR 32M for an excessively intrusive employee monitoring system in French warehouses using handheld scanners to track productivity in real-time, including a 'Stow Machine Gun' indicator flagging items scanned within 1.25 seconds, and idle-time tracking. The decision also found inadequate video surveillance information and insecure access controls. Note: the Conseil d'Etat reduced the fine to EUR 15M in December 2025.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1)(c), Art. 6(1)(f), Art. 12, Art. 13, Art. 32
