## BfDI v Vodafone — EUR 45 Million

**DPA:** BfDI (Germany, Federal)

**Target Entity:** Vodafone GmbH

**Fine Amount:** EUR 45,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2025-06-03

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

Germany's federal DPA imposed two fines totalling EUR 45M on Vodafone: EUR 15M for failing to monitor partner agencies (Art. 28), whose employees misused customer data to create fictitious contracts, and EUR 30M for security deficiencies in the MeinVodafone portal authentication process (Art. 32). The case sets a major precedent for controller obligations in managing third-party sales partners.

### Violated Articles

Art. 28(1), Art. 32(1)
