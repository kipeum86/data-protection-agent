## CNIL v Facebook Ireland — EUR 60 Million (Cookies)

**DPA:** CNIL (France)

**Target Entity:** Facebook Ireland Limited

**Fine Amount:** EUR 60,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2021-12-31

**Significance:** important

### Summary

Alongside the EUR 150M Google fine, CNIL fined Facebook EUR 60M on the same day for the identical violation on facebook.com: making it significantly more difficult for users to reject cookies than to accept them. Users could accept all cookies in one click but needed multiple actions to refuse. The paired decisions sent a clear signal that asymmetric cookie consent interfaces constitute unlawful dark patterns.

### Violated Articles

Art. 82 French Data Protection Act (ePrivacy transposition)
