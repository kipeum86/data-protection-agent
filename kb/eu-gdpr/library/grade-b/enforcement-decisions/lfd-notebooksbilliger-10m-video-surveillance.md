## LfD Lower Saxony v Notebooksbilliger.de — EUR 10.4 Million

**DPA:** LfD Niedersachsen (Lower Saxony, Germany)

**Target Entity:** notebooksbilliger.de AG

**Fine Amount:** EUR 10,400,000

**Decision Date:** 2021-01-08

**Significance:** important

### Summary

The LfD Lower Saxony fined notebooksbilliger.de EUR 10.4M for conducting blanket video surveillance of employees and customers for over two years without a specific lawful basis, retaining recordings for up to 60 days. The decision established that video surveillance can only target specific suspected individuals and must be time-limited. Note: the OLG Celle subsequently reduced the fine to EUR 900,000 on appeal.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1)(a), Art. 5(1)(c), Art. 5(1)(e), Art. 6(1)
