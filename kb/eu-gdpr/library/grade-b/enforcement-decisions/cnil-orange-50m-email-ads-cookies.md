## CNIL v Orange — EUR 50 Million

**DPA:** CNIL (France)

**Target Entity:** Orange SA

**Fine Amount:** EUR 50,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2024-11-14

**Significance:** important

### Summary

CNIL fined Orange EUR 50M for inserting unsolicited advertisements in the inboxes of 7.89 million email users without consent, and for continuing to read cookies after users withdrew consent. The decision relied on the CJEU ruling (Case C-102/20) that advertising messages displayed in email form constitute direct marketing requiring prior consent under the ePrivacy Directive.

### Violated Articles

Art. L. 34-5 French Postal and Electronic Communications Code, Art. 82 French Data Protection Act
