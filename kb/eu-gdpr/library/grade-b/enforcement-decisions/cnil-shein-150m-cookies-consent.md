## CNIL v Shein — EUR 150 Million

**DPA:** CNIL (France)

**Target Entity:** Infinite Styles Services Co. Limited (Shein)

**Fine Amount:** EUR 150,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2025-09-01

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

CNIL fined Shein EUR 150M for placing cookies on users' devices before they interacted with consent banners, providing incomplete information in cookie banners, and failing to identify third parties placing cookies. Approximately 12 million monthly visitors in France were affected. The decision underscores that cookie consent must be genuinely obtained before any tracking begins.

### Violated Articles

Art. 82 French Data Protection Act (ePrivacy transposition)
