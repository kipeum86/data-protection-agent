## Garante v TIM — EUR 27.8 Million

**DPA:** Garante per la protezione dei dati personali (Italy)

**Target Entity:** TIM S.p.A. (Telecom Italia)

**Fine Amount:** EUR 27,802,946

**Decision Date:** 2020-01-15

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

The Garante fined TIM EUR 27.8M for systematic unlawful telemarketing — millions of unsolicited calls without consent, failure to honor opt-out requests, and excessive data retention. The decision cited violations across nearly every major GDPR principle and established that aggressive telemarketing practices violate not only consent requirements but also accountability and data protection by design obligations.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1), Art. 5(2), Art. 6, Art. 7, Art. 21, Art. 24, Art. 25, Art. 28, Art. 32, Art. 33
