## Garante v Enel Energia — EUR 79.1 Million

**DPA:** Garante per la protezione dei dati personali (Italy)

**Target Entity:** Enel Energia S.p.A.

**Fine Amount:** EUR 79,100,000

**Decision Date:** 2024-02-08

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

The Garante imposed Italy's largest-ever GDPR fine of EUR 79.1M on Enel Energia for failing to control partner agencies that unlawfully conducted telemarketing using forged contracts and identification. At least 9,300 contracts were acquired from four unauthorized companies. The decision establishes that controllers bear full accountability for processing by their agents, even when those agents act without authorization.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(2), Art. 25, Art. 28
