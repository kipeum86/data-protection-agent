## AP Netherlands v Clearview AI — EUR 30.5 Million

**DPA:** AP (Netherlands)

**Target Entity:** Clearview AI Inc.

**Fine Amount:** EUR 30,500,000

**Decision Date:** 2024-05-16

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

The Dutch DPA imposed EUR 30.5M plus penalty orders exceeding EUR 5M on Clearview AI for building a facial recognition database of over 30 billion images scraped from the internet without consent. The decision found violations across lawfulness, biometric data processing, transparency, and failure to appoint an EU representative. The AP also considered holding Clearview's executives personally liable — a first under GDPR.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1)(a), Art. 6(1), Art. 9(1), Art. 12(1), Art. 14(1), Art. 14(2), Art. 27(1)
