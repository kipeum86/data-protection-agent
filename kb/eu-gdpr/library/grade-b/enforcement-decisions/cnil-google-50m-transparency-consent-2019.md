## CNIL v Google — EUR 50 Million (Transparency 2019)

**DPA:** CNIL (France)

**Target Entity:** Google LLC

**Fine Amount:** EUR 50,000,000

**Decision Date:** 2019-01-21

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

The first major GDPR fine: CNIL fined Google EUR 50M for insufficient transparency in how it informed users about data processing for ad personalization, and for obtaining invalid consent to personalized advertising during Android device setup. Information was scattered across multiple pages requiring 5-6 actions to access. The Conseil d'Etat upheld the decision in its entirety.

### Violated Articles

Art. 6, Art. 12, Art. 13
