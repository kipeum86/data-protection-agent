                                  16          Defendant Google LLC (“Google”) is in the business of collecting internet users’ data. Of

                                  18   internet applications. Anibal Rodriguez, JulieAnna Muniz, and seven other named plaintiffs

                                  22   collection. This argument runs down two independent narrative tracks: in plaintiffs’ view,

                                  23   Google’s liability flows from both (i) Google technology that, when functioning as advertised in a

                                  26   users and app developers alike. Google denies the “secret” software’s existence, and now moves to

                                  27   dismiss on the theory that its challenged data practices enjoy the consent of all involved. For the

                                  28   reasons set forth herein, the motion is granted in part, and denied in part.
