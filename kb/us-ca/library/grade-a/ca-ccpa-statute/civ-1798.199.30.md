## Official Text

1798.199.30.

The agency board shall appoint an executive director who shall act in accordance with agency
policies and regulations and with applicable law. The agency shall appoint and discharge
officers, counsel, and employees, consistent with applicable civil service laws, and shall fix the
compensation of employees and prescribe their duties. The agency may contract for services
that cannot be provided by its employees.
